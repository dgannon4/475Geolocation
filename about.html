Barsema Hall

Barsema Hall sets the standard for the learning environment of the 21st century offering a place for College of Business students to learn, study, meet or just relax. The 144,000 square foot facility combines classrooms, computer labs, a 375-seat auditorium, specialized labs, faculty and staff offices, and spaces for studying, meeting and even a cafe.
